Create a clean, minimal, text-first website inspired by https://techessentials.in/

Website name: Ctrl + G  
Tagline: From Ctrl+V to Growth

Design & Style:
- Minimal, editorial blog-style layout
- Lots of white space
- Text-focused (ideas over visuals)
- Use Inter font
- Black / dark gray text on white background
- Large readable headings
- Calm, professional, publication-like feel
- No flashy animations or gradients

Pages & Sections:

1. Home Page
- Show website name "Ctrl + G"
- Tagline below it: "From Ctrl+V to Growth"
- Short intro paragraph:
  "Practical writing on technology, Excel, AI, and automation — for people who want smarter work, not more work."
- List of latest articles with:
  - Article title
  - Short description
  - Date / read time

2. Articles Page
- Heading: "Articles"
- Subtitle: "Writing on tech, Excel, AI & automation."
- Clean vertical list of articles similar to TechEssentials
- Clicking an article opens a full article page
- Article page should be distraction-free (no sidebar)

3. About Page
- Dedicated About page (not just a section)
- Heading: "About Ctrl + G"
- Content tone should be calm and thoughtful
- Include text similar to:
  "Most people don’t need more tools. They need clarity.
   Ctrl + G exists to help people move from copying workflows blindly
   to growing intentionally — using technology that actually works
   in real jobs."
- Use bullet points for what readers will gain:
  - Learn what to apply immediately
  - Know what to ignore
  - Think clearly in the age of AI

4. Header / Navigation
- Simple top navigation with links:
  - Home
  - Articles
  - About
- No heavy buttons, keep it minimal

5. Footer
- Minimal footer with:
  - Newsletter signup section:
    Heading: "Get new articles in your inbox."
    Email input field
    Subscribe button
  - Footer text:
    © 2026 Ctrl + G
    From Ctrl+V to Growth
  - Links:
    - Privacy Policy
    - Unsubscribe (or Manage subscription)

6. Privacy Policy Page
- Simple privacy policy page
- States that emails are collected only for article notifications
- No spam, unsubscribe anytime
- Clean, readable text

General Notes:
- The site should feel like a thoughtful publication, not a marketing website
- Prioritize readability and clarity
- No popups
- No ads
- Calm, confident tone everywhere
